What most PMs miss

Most PMs are fluent in about eight to ten charts and unaware the others exist. The biggest gaps are EVM curves, Tornado diagrams and Control charts — the three tools most associated with senior programme management. Knowing when to use which chart matters more than knowing all of them.

The expert tip

Filter to Often missed and work through those charts first. The Tornado diagram and Control chart are used infrequently but have outsized impact in budget negotiations and steering committees. Being the PM who produces a Tornado diagram in a risk conversation immediately signals a different level of capability.
